TACC Media Complaints Collector - Help

The TACC MCC was created so the trans community and allies could make complaints to media companies and upload those complaints to TACC website via this plugin. Most media companies refuse to tell us the number of complaints they get or what those complaints are. This tool will allow the community to fight back.

The only media company this plugin currently works with is the BBC.

Submitting a complaint and saving the data

To submit a complaint you need to fill out the BBC complaints website at the following address. BBC Make a Complaint

Follow the instruction on the BBC website and submit your complaint. The MCC will have now opened a new page shown below.

There's two main parts of the submission page you're interested in. The first red box above highlights the optional data that you can decline to send. If this data is omitted the data will be anonymised. The second red box is the data that will be sent to the TACC website.

To send the data press the green "Send to TACC" button.

Once the data has been sent and assuming an error hasn't occurred the following will be displayed.

Again there are two important parts to the sent data page. The first red box shown above is a warning you must go to your email and click on the BBC confirmation email otherwise your complaint will be discard. You have an approximately 60 minutes in which to do this.

The second red box shows the status of the data sent and a unique complaint number.

MCC Dashboard and finding a previous complaint number

The MCC Dashboard can be found on the toolbar top right and is designated with in the trans colours. If the icon is missing from chrome, click the jigsaw piece and find the TACC Media Complaints Collector and click the pin to toolbar icon.

Click the icon and the dashboard will open as shown below.

Note the "Check Problematic Stories" feature is not currently implemented, but will be coming soon.

Top right of the dashboard you will find a link that will open this help file.

In the bottom panel "Stored Complaint" you will find the complaints you have submitted to TACC. The TACC Complaint Number is a clickable link which will open the Media Complaint Response Form on the TACC website and display the full data as submitted by yourself, see below for more details.

The blue delete button will remove the record from the local machine but not the website. This is so you can remove complaints that have either been fully actioned through the BBC complaints procedure.

Note if you have anonymised your data and you do not keep a record of the TACC Complaint Number, TACC cannot remove the complaints you have submitted.

Submitting replies from the media company

To submit a reply from the BBC, which is extremely helpful in our attempts to hold them to task. Click on the the TACC Complaint Number in the MCC Dashboard and the TACC Website will open with your record displayed, see below.

The media complaints response form will display the data you originally submitted with your complaint in the green Complaint Details box, below you will find the Existing Replies box.

In the first red box you center the BBC provided reference number if you so wish. The reference number does give TACC the ability to prove the complaint was made, IT DOES NOT give us the ability to learn your personal details.

The next red box is the complaint response. You can paste the email response from the BBC here. We would like to know their response even if it's a boilerplate response. You can also add your own notes as you please.

The last red box is a checkbox, you must agree with the statement you have understood our privacy policy linked on the same line, a more easily read version is below, and tick the box to be a able to submit the record.

When you submit the record you will be asked if you wish to submit another. If you answer no but want to check the entered data select NO and then perform a page refresh.

TACC's Privacy Policy

The full TACC privacy policy is available at CLICK HERE.

TACC will not provide your personal data to anyone full stop. If you have provided your personal details you can request to remove that data from our system at any point we guarantee to carry out the deletion within 7 working days. If you have the TACC Complaint Number we will also delete any complaints you have entered into our system even if it's anonymised.

To contact us you can either email us direct at info@tacc.org.uk or thorough out website mail form.
