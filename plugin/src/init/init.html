TACC Media Complaints Collector

Please read our human friendly privacy policy before using this tool, you must agree to the policy before the tool will be enabled:

Policy

By abiding to our privacy policy you agree that TACC can collect the form data from your BBC complaint and return the data to our servers. This data will be used for research by TACC. We will not share this data outside TACC without your written consent, unless the data you provide is anonymous.

Personal Data

Your personal data can consist of your first, last name and email address.
You can opt in or out of providing this information on the "Sending Data to TACC" confirmation page. See the help page for further details.

Removal of your data from our servers

If you have provided us with anonymised data you must be able to provide either the "TACC Record ID" or a "BBC Reference Number" for the complaint records you wish us to delete. Without either of these numbers we will be unable to locate your complaint. A list of "TACC Record ID's" are available from the TACC pop down located on the toolbar above.

Please note: You are able to delete a "TACC Record ID" from this extension once this has been done there is no recovery method. See the help page for further details.

If you have provided your personal details please provide these when requesting to remove your data.

Please use our email form on our website to request removal of your data.
